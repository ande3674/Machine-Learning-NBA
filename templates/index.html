Use NBA Data To Predict A Win


Welcome to our NBA game predictor!

An NBA game has 5 players on each team that can be substituted throughout a 48 minute game. Using machine learning with players' physical attributes and age over thousands of games, come try your hand leading a team to victory!

You can pick any players on the current roster of each team and allow them to play up to 48 minutes per player. Remember to pick at least 5 players and have fun!

  1. Select a NBA Home team and Visiting team
  2. Input Player Time in minutes for any player on each team (48 minutes max per player)
  3. Keep adding players and/or time until total team time is 240 minutes per side
  4. Click on Predict to see which team will win!!
